Georgia Power Bill Calculator

The utility bill calculator is a resource that the Commision is making available that allows a consumer to check the accuracy of their montly electric bill. A bill calculator has been prepared for customers of the Georgia Power Company. This bill calculator applies only to the residential customers who are receiving service under the standard Residential Tariff (R-27). For more details regarding Georgia Power's residential tariff offerings, please visit Georgia Power Pricing & Rates
